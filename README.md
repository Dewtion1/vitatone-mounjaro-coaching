## How to Make It Live (GitHub Pages)
1. Go to your repository **Settings** → **Pages**
2. Under "Build and deployment":
   - **Source**: Deploy from a branch
   - **Branch**: `main`
   - **Folder**: `/ (root)`
3. Click **Save**

Your beautiful coaching site will be publicly accessible in ~1 minute!
